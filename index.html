Round Stats

HI — Handicap Index
Your portable handicap number. Lower = better. Calculated from the best 8 of your last 20 rounds.
Net Score
Your gross score minus your Course Handicap. A net score near par means you played to your ability.
FIR — Fairway in Regulation
Tee shot lands on the fairway. Tracked on par 4s and 5s only.
GIR — Green in Regulation
Ball on the putting surface in (par − 2) strokes or fewer.
PEN — Penalty Strokes
Total penalty strokes taken (water hazards, out of bounds, etc.).
UD — Up & Down
Missed the green in regulation but still made par or better — chip/pitch + one putt.
X-UD — Missed Up & Down
Missed the green in regulation and took more than one putt to hole out.
PUTTS
Total putts for the round. Tour average is around 29. Under 30 is solid.
